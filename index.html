About Us

We've beem making healthy food last for 10 years

Pakashala is a pure vegetarian restaurant located in Namma Bengaluru, specializing in North Indian, South Indian, and Italian cuisines. With a focus on customer satisfaction, the restaurant provides high-quality, hygienic, and delicious food options, as well as a variety of dining experiences to suit every need.

North Indian cuisine is known for its rich and flavorful dishes, South Indian cuisine is known for its lighter and healthier dishes, Italian cuisine is known for its delicious pasta dishes, pizzas, and risottos.

Food Menu

Masala Dose

Masala dosa is a popular South Indian dish that originated in the state of Karnataka. It is served with potato curry, chutneys, and sambar.

Price: ₹ 100

Idli Vada

Idli are round, steamed cakes, Vada are savory, fried snacks that look like fried doughnuts. Idli vada is often served with: Spicy hot sambar and Coconut chutney

Price: ₹ 100

Paneer kulcha

Paneer kulcha is a popular Indian dish that is a soft and fluffy roti filled with paneer and spices. It is a delicious and hearty meal that is often served with gravy.

Price: ₹ 200

Paneer tikka

Paneer tikka is a popular Indian dish made with cubes of paneer that are marinated in a spicy mixture of spices and then grilled. It is a delicious and hearty dish that is often served with naan or roti.

Price: ₹ 200

Aglio e olio

Aglio e olio is a simple Italian sauce that's usually served with pasta. The name translates to "garlic and oil".

Price: ₹ 300

Penne arrabbiata

Penne arrabbiata is a pasta dish made with a spicy, tomato-based sauce that originated in the Lazio region of Italy.

Price: ₹ 300
